Immersive VR Art Gallery Tour

Overview

This project is a fully immersive virtual reality (VR) experience that allows users to explore a digital art gallery. As the lead XR developer, I focused on creating a believable and engaging environment where users can freely move around and interact with the artwork. The goal was to leverage VR technology to make art more accessible and engaging.

Key Features

  • Realistic Environment: A carefully designed 3D gallery space with realistic lighting and spatial audio.
  • Interactive Exhibits: Users can approach paintings and sculptures to view them up close and read information about the art and artist.
  • VR Locomotion: Implemented intuitive movement controls for a comfortable and seamless VR experience.

Technologies Used

Game Engine: Unity
3D Modeling: Blender
Target Platform: VR Headsets (e.g., Oculus Quest)
